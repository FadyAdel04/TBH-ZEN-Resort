About Us


ZEN Resort Sahl Hasheesh

TBH is a hospitality entity that owns both resorts & restaurants based in Hurghada, Egypt. We mainly present the idea of Boutique Hotels in Hurghada, merging quality with service.

Company Vision

Our ambition is to create customer loyalty based on guests who are satisfied with our services. We aim to expand TBH hotels, resorts & restaurants all over Egypt with the same consistent quality standards.

Company Mission

We innovate and create quality standards for our customers, ensuring a memorable experience through the quality of our products and services.
